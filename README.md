## How to Play
1. Click the link above.
2. Enter your name.
3. Read the story card (nouns in blue, verbs in red, etc.) and tap one of the two buttons.
4. Check the grammar key at the bottom: Nouns: Blue, Verbs: Red, Adjectives: Green, Adverbs: Purple.
